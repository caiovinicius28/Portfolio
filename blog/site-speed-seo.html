Why site speed matters for SEO and sales

Vyzion Systems · Digital growth team

Slow pages lose rankings and customers. How Vyzion optimizes performance, Core Web Vitals and mobile UX to drive more conversions.

Site speed is not a technical detail — it directly affects rankings and revenue. Every extra second of load time increases bounce rate and kills conversions.

Why speed is a revenue lever, not a tech detail

At Vyzion, we optimize Core Web Vitals, images, scripts and asset delivery so your pages load fast on mobile and desktop. Fast sites rank higher and convert better.

If your site is slow, you are paying for traffic that never sees your offer. A performance audit is the first step to recover leads and Google positions.

What we optimize on every Vyzion build

  • Core Web Vitals: LCP under 2.5s, stable layout, fast interaction
  • Image and video delivery: modern formats, lazy loading, CDN-ready assets
  • Script diet: only what converts — analytics, chat and tags loaded intelligently

Google uses page experience as a ranking signal. Shoppers abandon slow mobile pages before your offer loads. A performance audit often reveals quick wins — oversized images, render-blocking scripts, bloated plugins — that recover rankings and revenue within weeks.
