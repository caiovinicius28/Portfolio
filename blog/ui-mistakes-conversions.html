7 UI mistakes that kill conversions (and how to fix them)

Vyzion Systems · Digital growth team

Slow loads, weak hierarchy and buried CTAs cost you sales every day.

UI mistakes cost sales every day: slow loads, confusing hierarchy, hidden CTAs and forms that are too long.

UX mistakes that silently drain revenue

Fix visual hierarchy so the eye goes straight to value and action. Reduce form fields to essentials. Make sure primary buttons stand out on mobile.

  • Weak visual hierarchy — users can't find the primary action
  • Forms with too many fields or unclear error states
  • Slow interactions and layout shift on mobile
Small UX fixes often outperform big rebrands. Clarity converts.
